Business Scenario

An e-commerce company has grown rapidly but knows very little about its customers.

Management wants answers to questions like:

-Who are the highest-value customers?
-Which customers are likely to stop purchasing?
-Which regions generate the most revenue?
-Which products perform best?
-Which customers should receive loyalty rewards?
-Which customers should receive discount campaigns?

Your role is to analyze the transactional data and provide actionable insights.
